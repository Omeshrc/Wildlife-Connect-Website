Our Vision

To stand as the premier platform for discovery, learning, and uniting with passionate advocates, fostering a global community dedicated to the preservation of wildlife.

Our platform is carefully designed to support both wildlife lovers and conservationists, offering an interactive space for learning and engagement.

With Wildlife Connect, users step into a world where they can accurately identify animals, access extensive information, and connect with like-minded individuals. Beyond simply recognizing animals, our wildlife app acts as a vibrant center for conservationists to communicate, discuss, and socialize about wildlife conservation efforts in Sri Lanka.

Discover local projects, dive into the diverse world of Sri Lankan wildlife, regardless of where you are. Find new species, work together with other enthusiasts, and get involved in conservation efforts.

Features of the App

  • Wildlife Image Identification System
  • Report Wildlife Crimes
  • Information Library of Wildlife Animals
  • Display Recent Sightings of Wildlife
  • Post and Discuss Findings on the app's in-built Social Media Platform
